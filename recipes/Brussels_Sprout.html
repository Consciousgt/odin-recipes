Brussels Sprout Slaw with Cranberries

Description

Similar to a coleslaw, but made with shredded Brussels sprouts instead of cabbage for a unique taste!

This really does taste like coleslaw, so those Brussels sprouts haters out there will never know they're in there," says lutzflcat. "I love the little punch of sweetness and texture you get from the dried cranberries.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, shredded
  • 4 carrots, peeled and shredded
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ⅓ cup canola oil
  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon celery seed
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 5 ounces dried cranberries

Steps in Preparing Dish

  1. Combine Brussels sprouts, carrots, and cabbage in a large bowl.
  2. Combine salad dressing, mayonnaise, canola oil, vinegar, celery seed, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over vegetables and toss well to combine. Add dried cranberries and toss again.
